A bit about me.

I'm quiet at first and pretty genuine once you get to know me. I love the craft of design — the UI details, the copy on screens, the little things people don't notice until they're wrong. I've been told I'm good with UI, which I take as a real compliment because I care about it a lot.

Most of my work has been in consumer experiences where design has to hold its own against commercial constraints, regulatory requirements and engineering realities. I like talking to customers, and I like going back to fix copy and flows after you've heard what they actually think.

Professionally: five years across fintech, insurance, energy and ed-tech. Currently at Marshmallow, previously at So Energy, with earlier contract work in ed-tech and brand design. I studied graphic design and social media marketing and graduated top of my class.

Outside of work: long walks, the gym, skincare rabbit holes, and a long-standing dream of one day living by the sea.

Always up for a good conversation.
